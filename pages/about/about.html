ABOUT US:

Business Click is a student-led organization supporting youth entrepreneurs globally ages 13-35. Business Click was founded in July 2021 by Zahrah Ahmed - a Canadian high school student who witnessed many small businesses failing during the pandemic due to a lack of knowledge about how to switch to an online platform. Similarly, many businesses were being created by youth - but they had no knowledge of how to maintain a business. Business Click supports small businesses owned by youth through educational events on various topics, accessible skill guides, funding, and more. We have had members, volunteers, and supported businesses from countries all over the world and all inhabited continents. Stay tuned for upcoming events and resources on our website and social media pages!
