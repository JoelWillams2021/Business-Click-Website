Social Media Marketing Workshop

Our first event held in March 2022 taught small businesses powerful social media strategies for free to expand their online presence brand, no matter what stage they were on. Build a Business

This workshop held in April of 2022 taught immigrant and youth entrepreneurs the power of sacrifice, and hard work to get to your business from the perspective of Payal Abhichanai, a six-figure immigrant business owner. Payal taught us all about the skills that helped her reach success - including digital marketing, hiring, and more.

Youth Leadership Conference

Our youth leadership conference taught youth how to be leaders in their communities through an entrepreneurial lens taught by established speakers from McMaster University, Sending Sunshine (a national nonprofit), and more. To exhibit their skills, Business Click also offered a range of three volunteer opportunities to youth to showcase their leadership in a local setting.

DAY 2:
